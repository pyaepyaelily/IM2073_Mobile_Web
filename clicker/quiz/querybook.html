about us

Best Candy in Singapore!

why choose us?

This charming candy store, which was opened in 2022, is the newest continuously running confectionery in Singapore. In 2021, Stephanie and Jia Hui took over the business and got to work restoring the building, which features stained glass, carved wood, and curved windows. Sweets are made on-site and include classic favorites such as buttercreams, clear toy candy, and penny candy, as well as modern treats including Gianduja and Chocolate Truffle.
